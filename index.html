HOW TO USE:

Times are tough during the pandemic but there are still things we can do to make connections and interact with others no matter how far away they are

Our app shows a visual of people connecting with another by through watching a movie generated through The Movie DB database

1: Select a movie of your choice base on the current top 200 most popular movies

2: Select the city you are in along with the city of the person you will be watching a movie in among our list of some of the most popular cities in the world

3: Hit the "Show Me My Date" button once you have made up your mind on the movie and location of your date!
